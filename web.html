EXPERIMENTAL WEB

OMNIPRESENT, Martin Guevara, 2018, website

OMNIPRESENTis a project from the Fall of 2018 where I use a website to host and simultaneously play live streams from around the world. These videos include live feeds from religious ceremonies, nature, and of cities across the world including Mexico City, London, Brooklyn, Sevilla, Hong Kong, and Santa Monica. Pedestrians unknowingly performed their daily and private lives for an anonymous audience.
