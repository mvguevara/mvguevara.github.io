HYPER PERMEANCE 2020, 3D scans

HYPER PERMEANCE (2020) was a zine I self published during the beginning of the global pandemic. I began exploring 3D scanning and its ability to capture the body in its true ephemeral form.

stop space return 2020, VR

stop space returnis a VR environment that holds old poetry, selfies, ideas, thoughts, and so on. This space attempts to transcend the idea of a virtual space as a collection of archived experiences.


poseNet.mistake2020, poseNet, WEBGL

poseNet.mistake uses PoseNet which is a vision model that can be used to estimate the pose of a person in an image or video by estimating where key body joints are. This augmented reality piece, reimagines the body as a responsible component for XR (extended reality).
