AUTOMATED, Martin Guevara, 2019, video

AUTOMATEDis a fantasy inspired by "A Cyborg Manifesto", one of Donna Haraway's most influential writings. This is also a music video created for ARCA, a Venezuelan recording artist. In the writing, Haraway uses the cyborg as a caveat and a critique of feminism but also calls for responsible bridges between animal, human and machine. The idea of humans as cyborg via medicine and technology are presented in the video. I attempt to mesh footage of automated sight from medical procedures and military use, both in which Haraway explains make us cyborgs. War being a machine and medicine being technology in which we use as a means to survive. This video was initially created to help me possibly understand the writing in a visual sense but has only broadened my own fantasy of the cyborg identity.

Expensive, Martin Guevara, 2019, video

This video was commisioned by music recording artistSaxon Villain 2019. We collaborated through the internet to conjure the spirit of late 90's LA culture. This video depicts the gang-life of East Los Angeles while highlighting the multiple tiers of sub-cultures within those neighborhoods.

Flower Fuel, Martin Guevara, 2019, video

This music video was in collaboration with recording artist Derek Lopez, also known by his stage name BOMBOY. The video is dedicated to an art collective known as Flower Fuel who, through art, imagine a cyber-botanical future. In this video I use the name Flower Fuel to guide the visual story of the collective and their mission.
